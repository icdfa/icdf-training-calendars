# School of Internship Training (SIT)

## Overview

The School of Internship Training (SIT) at the International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensic Academy (ICDF Academy) provides immersive internship programs that combine theoretical knowledge with practical, real-world experience. Our programs prepare participants for professional roles through intensive hands-on training and mentorship.

## Mission

To provide comprehensive internship training programs that bridge the gap between academic knowledge and professional practice in cybersecurity and digital forensics.

## Programs Offered

### Ethical Hacking and Digital Forensics Internship

An intensive internship program providing hands-on experience in ethical hacking techniques, penetration testing, and digital forensics investigation. Participants work on real-world scenarios under the guidance of experienced professionals.

**Program Details:**
- **Duration:** Multiple phases (Phase 1, Phase 2, etc.)
- **Format:** Internship with practical projects
- **Delivery:** Hands-on training with mentorship
- **Certification:** Ethical Hacking and Digital Forensics Internship Certificate

**Current Cohorts:**

## Key Features

### Real-World Experience
Participants work on actual projects and scenarios encountered in professional cybersecurity roles.

### Mentorship
Each intern is paired with experienced cybersecurity professionals for guidance and support.

### Practical Skills Development
Focus on developing hands-on skills with industry-standard tools and techniques.

### Professional Networking
Opportunities to connect with industry professionals and build career networks.

## Admission Requirements

Prospective interns should have:
- Foundational knowledge in cybersecurity or related field
- Strong interest in ethical hacking and digital forensics
- Commitment to full-time or intensive part-time participation
- Ability to work independently and in teams
