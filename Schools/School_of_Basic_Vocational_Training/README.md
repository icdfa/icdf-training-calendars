# School of Basic Vocational Training (SBVT)

## Overview

The School of Basic Vocational Training (SBVT) at the International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensic Academy (ICDF Academy) provides foundational training programs designed to introduce professionals to cybersecurity and digital forensics. Our programs offer comprehensive, practical training that prepares participants for entry-level and intermediate roles in the cybersecurity industry.

## Mission

To deliver high-quality foundational training in cybersecurity and digital forensics, equipping professionals with essential skills and knowledge to begin their careers in information security.

## Programs Offered

### Fellowship in Web Application Security and Digital Forensics

A comprehensive fellowship program combining web application security principles with digital forensics fundamentals, preparing participants for roles in web security assessment and incident response.

**Program Details:**
- **Duration:** Multiple phases (Phase 1, Phase 2, etc.)
- **Format:** Practical, hands-on training
- **Delivery:** Intensive courses with real-world scenarios
- **Certification:** Fellowship in Web Application Security and Digital Forensics

## Key Features

### Foundational Training
Programs focus on building strong foundational knowledge in cybersecurity principles and digital forensics.

### Practical Application
Emphasis on hands-on training with real-world scenarios and industry-standard tools.

### Career Preparation
Training designed to prepare participants for entry-level and intermediate cybersecurity roles.

### Expert Instruction
Courses taught by experienced cybersecurity professionals with industry expertise.

## Admission Requirements

Prospective students should have:
- Basic computer literacy
- Interest in cybersecurity and digital forensics
- Commitment to professional development
- Ability to complete intensive training programs
