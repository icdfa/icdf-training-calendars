# School of Advanced Vocational Training

## Overview

The School of Advanced Vocational Training (SAVT) at the International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensic Academy (ICDF Academy) is dedicated to providing comprehensive, practical training in advanced cybersecurity and digital forensics disciplines. Our programs are designed to equip professionals with the skills, knowledge, and certifications needed to excel in specialized cybersecurity roles.

## Mission

To deliver world-class advanced vocational training in cybersecurity and digital forensics, preparing professionals to investigate complex cybercrimes, analyze digital evidence, and protect critical infrastructure.

## Programs Offered

The School of Advanced Vocational Training currently offers the following programs:

## Key Features

### Practical Training
All courses emphasize hands-on, practical training with industry-standard tools and real-world scenarios.

### Expert Instruction
Courses are taught by experienced cybersecurity professionals and forensic experts with years of field experience.

### Case Study Integration
Programs incorporate real-world case studies to provide practical application of theoretical knowledge.

### Industry-Standard Tools
Students work with professional-grade forensic and investigation software used in the industry.

### Flexible Learning
Training is designed to accommodate working professionals with intensive, focused course schedules.

## Admission Requirements

Prospective students should have:

- Basic understanding of computer systems and networks
- Interest in cybersecurity and digital forensics
- Commitment to professional development
- Ability to complete intensive training programs
