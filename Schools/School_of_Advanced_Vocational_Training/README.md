# School of Advanced Vocational Training (SAVT)

## Overview

The School of Advanced Vocational Training (SAVT) at the International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensic Academy (ICDF Academy) is dedicated to providing comprehensive, advanced training in specialized cybersecurity and digital forensics disciplines. Our programs are designed to equip professionals with expert-level skills, knowledge, and certifications needed to excel in advanced cybersecurity roles.

## Mission

To deliver world-class advanced vocational training in cybersecurity and digital forensics, preparing professionals to investigate complex cybercrimes, analyze digital evidence, conduct threat intelligence operations, and protect critical infrastructure.

## Programs Offered

The School of Advanced Vocational Training offers the following advanced programs:

### Advanced SOC Analyst

An advanced program focused on developing expertise in Security Operations Center (SOC) operations, threat detection, incident response, and security monitoring.

**Program Details:**
- **Duration:** Multiple phases (Phase 1, Phase 2, etc.)
- **Format:** Practical, hands-on training
- **Delivery:** Intensive courses with real-world scenarios
- **Certification:** Advanced SOC Analyst

**Current Cohorts:**
- [Batch 2025](Advanced_SOC_Analyst/Batch_2025/)

### Advanced Cybercrime Investigations

A comprehensive program focused on developing expertise in investigating cybercrime cases, analyzing digital evidence, and understanding the legal and ethical frameworks governing cybercrime investigations.

**Program Details:**
- **Duration:** Multiple phases (Phase 1, Phase 2, etc.)
- **Format:** Practical, hands-on training
- **Delivery:** Intensive courses with real-world case studies
- **Certification:** Advanced Cybercrime Investigator

**Current Cohorts:**
- [Batch 2025](Advanced_Cybercrime_Investigations/Batch_2025/)

### Advanced Cybersecurity Operations and Threat Intelligence

An advanced program combining cybersecurity operations with threat intelligence analysis, preparing professionals to identify, analyze, and respond to sophisticated cyber threats.

**Program Details:**
- **Duration:** Multiple phases (Phase 1, Phase 2, etc.)
- **Format:** Practical, hands-on training
- **Delivery:** Intensive courses with threat intelligence exercises
- **Certification:** Advanced Cybersecurity Operations and Threat Intelligence Specialist

**Current Cohorts:**
- [Batch 2025](Advanced_Cybersecurity_Operations_and_Threat_Intelligence/Batch_2025/)

## Key Features

### Advanced Training
All programs provide expert-level training in specialized cybersecurity domains.

### Practical Training
Courses emphasize hands-on, practical training with industry-standard tools and real-world scenarios.

### Expert Instruction
Courses are taught by experienced cybersecurity professionals and forensic experts with years of field experience.

### Case Study Integration
Programs incorporate real-world case studies to provide practical application of theoretical knowledge.

### Industry-Standard Tools
Students work with professional-grade forensic and investigation software used in the industry.

### Flexible Learning
Training is designed to accommodate working professionals with intensive, focused course schedules.

## Admission Requirements

Prospective students should have:
- Intermediate to advanced understanding of cybersecurity concepts
- Professional experience in cybersecurity or related field
- Strong interest in specialized cybersecurity domains
- Commitment to professional development
- Ability to complete intensive training programs

For specific program requirements, please refer to individual program pages.
