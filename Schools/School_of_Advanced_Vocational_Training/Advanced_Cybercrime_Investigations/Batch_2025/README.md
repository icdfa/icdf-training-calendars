This cohort represents a diverse group of cybersecurity professionals committed to advancing their expertise in cybercrime investigation and digital forensics.

## Phase 2 Highlights

Phase 2 is the advanced phase of the program featuring:

### Four Core Courses
1. **ACI902** - Cyber Forensic Data Analysis (4 units)
2. **ACI903** - Legal and Ethical Aspects of Cybercrime (4 units)
3. **ACI901** - Cybercrime Investigations Case Studies (4 units)
4. **ACI904** - Capstone Project in Cybercrime Investigations (5 units)

### Five Integrated Case Studies
1. The E-Commerce Data Breach
2. The Insider Threat
3. The Ransomware Attack
4. The Phishing Campaign
5. The Dark Web Marketplace

### Capstone Project
A comprehensive, simulated cybercrime investigation integrating all skills and knowledge acquired throughout the program.
