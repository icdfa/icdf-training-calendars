## Version Format

Version numbers follow the format: MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH

- **MAJOR**: Significant structural changes or complete reorganization
- **MINOR**: Addition of new schools, programs, or major features
- **PATCH**: Updates to existing calendars, bug fixes, or minor improvements

## Change Categories

- **Added**: New features, schools, programs, or documentation
- **Changed**: Updates to existing content or structure
- **Deprecated**: Features or content marked for removal
- **Removed**: Deleted features, schools, or programs
- **Fixed**: Bug fixes or corrections
- **Security**: Security-related updates
